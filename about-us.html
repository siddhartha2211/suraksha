About Us

Welcome to SURAKSH, where our mission is to provide education and awareness on personal safety for children. Our team is dedicated to creating a safe and supportive online environment where children can learn about boundaries, good and bad touch, and the importance of communication.

We believe in the power of education to empower children and their families, creating a community that understands the significance of personal safety. Together, we can make a positive impact on the lives of many.

Thank you for being a part of the SURAKSH community!
